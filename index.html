I am an Associate Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Automation and Ramanujan Fellow at the Indian Institute of Science. >Before joining IISc, I was a post-doctoral scholar at Caltech. I obtained my Ph.D. in Computer Science at the University of Wisconsin-Madison.

Research: My interests lie in the design, analysis, and applications of algorithms. My current research focuses on algorithmic game theory and approximation algorithms.
